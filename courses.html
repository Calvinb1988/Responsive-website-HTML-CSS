Antenatal Courses

Prepare for your baby's birth and first few weeks with our 4 week in-depth antenatal course which runs from Tuesday to Thursdays weekly, or join a weekend short course with other soon-to-be parents.

Breastfeeding Support

Get the best feeding advice and lactation support from our IBCLC registered midwife

Baby & Me milestone groups

Join our Friday weekly milestone and age-appropriate activity classes to boost your baby's emotional and physical development.

Track your baby's weight

Visit the clinic to have your baby weighed and measured by our midwife to keep track of your baby's growth.

Parenting Workshops

Starting solids? Want to learn baby massage? Need feeding advice? Feeling a bit blue? Join one of our various workshops and seminars to guide yourself towards more confident, gentle parenting.

Infant CPR and First Aid Courses

Get the confidence you need in an emergency situation. We cover CPR, first aid, burns, allergies and poisoning. Suitable for parents, caregivers and teachers. Presented by SafeKids.

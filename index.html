Courses We Offer

Come and join us for courses ranging from new borns all the way up to young toddlers

View our full courses page to see more!

New Borns

We offer a number of courses for new moms. These classes include giving guidance on breastfeeding and formula feeding. We also give lessons that starts your baby working towards their milestones early.

Infants

As your baby gets older we look at engaging them in a number of classes which focuses on movement and co-ordination. We also start looking at messy and sensory play to get your babies minds active and exploring.

Toddlers

As you babies move up to toddlers we look at engaging them in more physical activities which strengthen their movement and motor skills which is essential as they start learning to work and talk.
